Developer, Endurant Student , Explorer

I am a hobbyist software developer and a tinkerer.

I am Anukul Kumar. I was born and brought up in suburb of Patna, India. Completed my higher education, obtaining a B-Tech in Computer Science from National Institute of Technology Patna.

Started my corporate journey from interning at Brightchamps in 2022, a ed-tech startup, focussing on imparting new age skills and knowledge to the next generation. Working in the backend team provided a top-view of how the system is implemented and the workflow and the exhilaration I got there will remain in a sweet spot inside me.

I believe that the world can have a better future if we allow ourselves to identify as one family (वसुधैव कुटुम्बकम). Trying to feel what others are going through and observe their prespective can help. Infact the path to unity starts within oneself.

I discovered the idea of code only after getting in the bachelor program. But soon, the world of code and logic started to fascinate me. Starting with C, I have found myself getting involved since. Instructing the computer to do a task (even for printing a star pattern :) ) was something that felt magical.

My interest also takes me frequently to the field of economics. I find myself learning about the macroscopic as well as microscopic factors impacting the economy.

We live in pretty exciting time in the world. Each day brings a lot of happenings, intersecting our lives in way unimaginable. I try to learn and record them as evident past for the future.

This website serves a purpose for me, to share my works, voice out my opinions and to connect with the broader mass.

That said, I have enjoyed experimenting & learning as a lifelong journey and hopefully continue to do so. The process of learning should not be bounded by pre-concieved notion and must allow for a free mind to grow.In times not occupied by work, I can be found reading a book, enjoying a classic movie, playing guitar or taking a stroll.
